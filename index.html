FAQ

Common questions.

How is SmartSwan different from a traditional systems integrator?

We take responsibility for outcomes, not staffing hours. Every engagement has named SLOs, and we run the systems we build so incentives stay aligned long after the initial delivery.

Can you work inside our existing cloud accounts?

Yes — that's our default. We operate under your identity provider, use your networking, and leave code, infrastructure, and documentation in your repositories.

Where is our data during AI deployments?

Inside your environment. Inference, retrieval, and evaluation all run in a VPC you control, with no data leaving for third-party model providers unless you explicitly opt in.

What does a typical engagement look like?

A short assessment, a fixed-scope pilot with clear success metrics, and then a rollout phase. Most first pilots land production value in six to ten weeks.

Do you support regulated industries?

Yes. We regularly work under HIPAA, SOC 2, ISO 27001, and India's DPDP framework, and we can produce the evidence trail auditors expect.
